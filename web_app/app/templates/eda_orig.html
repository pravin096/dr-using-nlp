Overview of Training Dataset

In summary:

  • This is an imbalanced dataset, irrespective of whether we consider each category/ label as a class, or each unique combination of labels as a class

Over 76% of the messages belong to one class

23% of messages have no labels, 21% only one amongst the 36 labels; a small % of messages have multiple - between 2 to 23 labels

Food, water, people, earthquake, flood are most referred words for messages with "related" and "aid related" labels
